INTELLIGENT MOBILE ROBOT FOR SMART FACTORY

Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, (Intern), December 2019- February 2020

Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology, August 2019- July 2020

Team Size: 4
Individual Role: Team Lead; handled image processing and developed a training dataset and test dataset
Objective: To design a Mobile Robot which aids in separating quality products from the dysfunctional products in a manufacturing unit
Tools used: PLC-Fx5u, HMI-GOT2000, Raspberry Pi3 B+, Gxworks, Python IDE, Servo, Motor-HG-KN23, Thingspeak, OpenCV, Tensorflow, Tkinter, Planetary Gear Box, Nema23 Stepper Motor
Application :The need for mobile robots is increasing day by day in industries, since it provides accurate results when compared to manual operation.Manual inspection of products in industries takes more time when compared with automatic inspection.Image processing is used to identify and separate the products based on pre-trained model.Tensorflow and OpenCV are used for fault identification.PLC(FX-5U) is used to control the robotic arm. HMI(GOT 2000) provides information about number of work pieces completed.
